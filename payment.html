Payment methods

Online payment

Payment by bank cards

Cities: All cities of Kazakhstan
How to pay: After requisition by
  • Debit cards of Kaspi Bank, Halyk Bank and other banks
  • Debit cards of Visa, Master Card, Cirrus Maestro, American Express
  • Credit cards PayDa, SmartCard
  • Bonus cards GoCard
Conditions:
  • To get a requisition you need an ID card

Payment via Kaspi QR

Cities: All cities of Kazakhstan
How to pay: After requisition
  • Scan QR-code, received after the requisition via Kaspi.kz app
  • Mobile version - via Kaspi.kz app, which will be opened after the requisition

Payment via QIWI

Cities: All cities of Kazakhstan
How to pay: After requisition
  • Via QIWI-terminal, specifying the phone number of your order
  • Via QIWI-wallet, specifying the phone number of your order
  • Via the site qiwi.com, choosing "Shopify" and specifying your phone number and order sum
Conditions
  • Payment is available only after changing the status to "Awaiting the payment"
  • To get an order, you need an ID card

Payment during getting an order

Payment by bank cards or QR at pickup points

Cities: Almaty, Astana, Karaganda, Pavlodar, Kostanay, Temirtau, Zhezkazgan, Aktobe, Rudnyi, Taraz, Shymkent
How to pay: After receiving the order via
  • Debit cards of Visa, Master Card
  • Credit cards Kaspi Red, Halyk Light, PayDa, SmartCard, Bank Center Credit
  • QR in kaspi.kz and HomeBank apps
Conditions:
  • Ask a manager if you can pay by this method at a pickup point

Cities: Almaty, Astana, Karaganda, Pavlodar, Kostanay, Temirtau, Zhezkazgan, Aktobe, Rudnyi, Taraz, Shymkent
How to pay: After receiving an order from a courier pay via a mobile POS-terminal by a bank card Visa or MasterCard
